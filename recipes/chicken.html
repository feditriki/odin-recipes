Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken Curry is a flavorful dish that blends tender chicken with aromatic spices and a rich, savory sauce. It’s a staple in many cuisines, especially South Asian, and can be enjoyed with rice or naan.

This version balances the warmth of spices like turmeric and cumin with the creaminess of coconut milk or yogurt, making it both comforting and delicious. It's a perfect dish for weeknight dinners or family meals.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast or thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 400ml coconut milk or 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the aromatics

    2. In a large pot, heat oil and sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant and golden.

  2. Add spices

    3. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, and cumin. Cook for 1–2 minutes to release their aroma.

  3. Brown the chicken

    4. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned on all sides.

  4. Add liquids

    5. Stir in tomato paste and coconut milk or yogurt. Season with salt and pepper.

  5. Simmer

    6. Let the curry simmer on low heat for 20–25 minutes until the chicken is tender and the sauce thickens.

  6. Garnish and serve

    7. Top with chopped coriander and serve with rice or naan.
